Cold Storage Doors

Bengal Rolling Shutters: Cold Storage Doors

Bengal Rolling Shutters offers the best solutions for the food sector and industries requiring cold storage between 0ºC (32°F) and -40ºC (-40°F). Our range of cold storage doors, including sliding, swing, roll-up, high-speed, and vertical lift doors, are ideal for maintaining optimal functional, climatic, hygienic, and sanitary conditions. These doors feature excellent thermal insulation and water tightness, ensuring efficient and reliable performance.

Types of Cold Storage Doors

01

Sliding Cold Storage Doors

Features
  • Ideal for large access areas or overhead passages.
  • Provides great thermal insulation and energy efficiency.
  • Option for insulated firewall available.
02

Swing Cold Storage Doors

Features
  • Multiple opening options: pulling, pushing, or turning the handle.
  • Resistant to scratches, chemicals, and high humidity.
  • Adjustable hinges in all three directions for precise fitting.
03

High-Speed Roll-Up Doors for Cold Storage

Features
  • Suitable for areas with high pedestrian and motorized traffic.
  • High humidity tolerance at access points.
  • Available in single canvas and double canvas models to prevent condensation and ice formation.
04

Vertical Lift Cold Storage Doors

Features
  • Designed for access areas with limited lateral space but ample vertical height.
  • Includes an anti-fall and anti-tip safety system patented by Bengal Rolling Shutters.
05

Controlled Atmosphere Doors

Features
  • Perfect for storage rooms, and fruit and vegetable ripening and storage at very low oxygen levels.
  • Equipped with a blade anti-fall and anti-tip system patented by Bengal Rolling Shutters.
  • CE marking for compliance with European standards.
06

Air Curtains for Cold Storage

Features
  • Specially designed for industrial cold rooms and freezers.
  • Offers significant energy savings, better conservation, reduced ice formation, and increased hygiene.
