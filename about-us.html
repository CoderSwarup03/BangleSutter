Company Profile

Bengal Rolling Shutters & Engg. Works was established in the year 1972 as a partnership firm at Howrah, West Bengal (India). We are engaged in manufacturing and supplying a diverse range of Rolling Shutters, Blinds, Sliding & Swinging Gates, Parking Barriers, Roofing Sheet, Roadway Safety Products & Building Construction Material. These products are highly appreciated for their durability, sturdy construction, dimensional accuracy, low maintenance and easy installation. Our products are a part of various commercial and non-commercial establishments across the country.

Our brilliant track record proves one thing that the company is backed with an efficient and dedicated team of professionals. We have appointed experienced professionals as heads of each department so that under their guidance all the operations are executed in an appropriate manner. These department heads also assist us in the recruitment process and thoroughly scrutinize the skills of interviewing candidates.

Quality procurement is very important to maintain high standards of production. Therefore, we have carefully short listed authentic market vendors, who offer us high end raw materials at market leading rates. The procured lot of raw materials is meticulously inspected by seasoned quality controllers before using them in the manufacturing processes.

We are empowered with a technologically advanced and spacious manufacturing unit. This unit boasts a large installation and production capacity. We have installed a diverse range of functionally efficient machines, which help us in manufacturing products in huge quantities and varied specifications. We offer an extensive array of products that are used in building construction work and thus, serve as a one-stop-shop for the same. Our qualitative ready-made building solution saves the hassles of running here and there for desired products. We make sure that the desired quantity of products is delivered at client's end within the set time frame.
