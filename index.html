Overview

Welcome To BENGAL ROLLING SHUTTERS & ENGG.WORKS

We take the opportunity to introduce BENGAL ROLLING SHUTTERS & ENGG.WORKS a private limited company in the business of Rolling shutter and manufacturing. Bengal Rolling Shutters(BRS ), a brand synonymous with Rolling Shutters in West Bengal and around is now in existence of 42nd year of service to the Shuttering Industry. Established in the year 1972, BRS, based in Kolkata is the pioneer in manufacturing the Quality Rolling shutters in the Region. Over four decades, BRS has coined its undisputed name and recognition in the market, catering to Small, Medium and Corporate organisations.
