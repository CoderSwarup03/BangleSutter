Fire Rated Rolling Shutter

Bengal Rolling Shutters: Fire Rated Rolling Shutter

Bengal Rolling Shutters’ fire rated rolling shutters are designed to meet high safety standards and provide reliable protection for large openings. These shutters are ideal for industrial installations, production facilities, electrical installations in high-rise buildings, and storage facilities where fire compartmentalization is essential.

With fire ratings ranging from 120 minutes up to 240 minutes, our fire rated rolling shutters are tested to ensure dependable performance during emergency conditions. Using certified shutters helps reduce fire risks, limit damage, and improve overall safety for people, property, and operations.

Standards & Certifications

01

Tested to BS 476 Part 20 and Part 22

Our rolling shutters are tested for stability and integrity in Warrington, ensuring they meet stringent safety requirements.

02

Certifire Certification

Our fire rolling shutters are certified under CF 5545, guaranteeing their reliability and effectiveness in fire protection.

Conclusion

Bengal Rolling Shutters’ fire rated rolling shutters deliver dependable protection, safety, and compliance for industrial, commercial, and high-rise applications. With proven performance and certified quality, they are an ideal solution for safeguarding premises against fire hazards.
